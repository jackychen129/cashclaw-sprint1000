# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to building a respectful, practical, and inclusive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and evidence-driven
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people
- Share reproducible data when challenging strategy claims
- Respect different backgrounds and markets

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Spam or manipulative promotion
- Encouraging deceptive business practices

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this code and may restrict participation for repeated violations.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and all project communication channels.
