# Skill: Sprint1000 Operator

## Purpose

Help a normal user earn the first **$1,000 in 30 days** through legal, ethical, execution-heavy service work using AI leverage.

## Operating constraints

- No scams, no fake testimonials, no spam blasts
- Keep actions practical: each step should be executable in less than 2 hours
- Prioritize cashflow over complexity

## Inputs to collect first

1. Current skills (even basic ones)
2. Available hours/day
3. Preferred market (local SMB, creators, freelancers, e-commerce)
4. Existing channels (LinkedIn, X, WeChat, Upwork, cold email list)

## Core loop (run daily)

1. Pick one micro-offer (single outcome, clear price, fast turnaround)
2. Generate 20 personalized outreach messages
3. Send outreach in approved channels
4. Handle replies and objections
5. Deliver paid work with AI-assisted SOPs
6. Ask for referral or testimonial after delivery

## Prompt templates

### Offer design

Design one micro-offer I can deliver in 48 hours.
My profile: {{profile}}
Market: {{market}}
Target buyer pain: {{pain}}
Return:
- Offer name
- Exact outcome
- Price tier (starter/standard/premium)
- 3 reasons buyer should trust this

### Outreach drafting

Write {{count}} short outreach messages for {{channel}}.
Offer: {{offer}}
Audience details: {{audience}}
Style: polite, clear, non-spammy.
Each message must include one personalized hook and one simple CTA.

### Delivery SOP

Build a delivery SOP for this paid task:
{{task}}
Constraints:
- I can spend {{hours}} hours
- I need quality high enough for referral
Return:
- Step-by-step checklist
- QA checklist
- Handoff template

## Weekly review process

Every 7 days, calculate:
- Outreach sent
- Replies received
- Calls booked
- Deals closed
- Revenue collected

Then adjust:
- Offer positioning
- Channel mix
- Pricing
- Fulfillment speed
